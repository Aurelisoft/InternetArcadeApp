What is the Internet Arcade APP?

It is an app that facilitates access to game content from the "Internet Archive" website.

According to information from its website, "Internet Archive" is: "a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, creating a digital library of websites and other cultural resources in digital format. Like a traditional library, we offer free access to researchers, historians, scholars, the visually impaired, and the general public. Our mission is to provide universal access to knowledge."

Con esta APP unicamente accedemos al contenido proporcionado por los enlaces compartidos desde el sitio wed de ARCHIVE.ORG y facilitamos su uso. No facilitamos la descarga y guardado del contenido para su distribución.

This app only provides access to content shared via links from the ARCHIVE.ORG website and facilitates its use. We do not facilitate the downloading and saving of content for distribution.

We are not responsible for how each user of the app uses the published content. The responsibility for sharing the content on other platforms rests with each user of the app.
