Internet Arcade APP Help

To play the games available on the Internet Arcade APP, you must first explore the folder corresponding to the desired platform.

Within each folder you will find a list of games corresponding to the platform or console.

Select the desired game; the buttons are already pre-configured.

Before playing, the desired game will be downloaded. In some cases, it may take a long time to load due to the download speed available on the Internet Archive website.

It is recommended to adjust the position of the movement and action buttons. Initially, the button to switch to adjustment mode will change color, indicating that the position has not been saved.

To play Arcade mode, you must first simulate inserting coins into the machine using the button called “Coin”, then you can press the “Start” button to start the game.

You can select the type of movement you want: with direction buttons or with a single joystick-type button.

Try playing vertically or horizontally, depending on your preference.

Activate automatic fire mode by repeatedly pressing the action button. After a few seconds, you can hold it down.

The list of games will increase if the app is well received.

Play and enjoy classic games.
